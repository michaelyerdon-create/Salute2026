About the Event

An Evening Supporting New York's Next Generation

Since 1858, YWCA NYC has worked to eliminate racism and advance opportunity for women, girls, and families across New York City. Salute is our signature annual celebration and fundraiser, bringing together hundreds of leaders from business, philanthropy, government, and community to honor those building pathways for New York's next generation.

Guests will gather at City Winery for a cocktail reception, seated dinner, awards program, and live paddle raise, with opportunities to meet the students, staff, and volunteers who shape our work.

What Salute Funds

Work That Reaches Families in Every Borough

Salute funds year-round programming that reaches students from kindergarten through early career across all five boroughs.

Provides K–8 students with academic support, hands-on enrichment in arts, music, cooking, and STEAM, trusted adult relationships, and a stable environment beyond the school day.

Prepares young women and gender-expansive youth to lead through advocacy, STEM, college and career readiness, entrepreneurship, and financial literacy. Each year, YWCA NYC also convenes the Girls Symposium, a citywide day that brings hundreds of girls together for leadership, wellness, college and career exploration, and youth-led advocacy.
